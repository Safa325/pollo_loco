Game Instructions

Welcome to the game! Here’s how to play:

Objective

  • Defeat the Chickens: Jump on top of chickens and small chickens to kill them.
  • Defeat the End Boss: Throw bottles at the end boss. You need to hit the end boss 5 times to defeat him.

El Pollo Loco

Game Instructions

Welcome to the game! Here’s how to play:

Objective

  • Defeat the Chickens: Jump on top of chickens and small chickens to kill them.
  • Defeat the End Boss: Throw bottles at the end boss. You need to hit the end boss 5 times to defeat him.

Controls

  • Move Left: Press the "A" key or the left arrow key.
  • Move Right: Press the "D" key or the right arrow key.
  • Jump: Press the "W" key or the up arrow key.
  • Throw: Press the spacebar.

Tips

  • Limited Bottles: You only have 5 bottles. Aim carefully when throwing them at the end boss.
  • Falling Direction: You must be falling to kill the chickens by jumping on them.
